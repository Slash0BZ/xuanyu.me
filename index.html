I am an assistant professor at Arizona State University, where I direct the ARC Lab.

My primary research interest is controllable and trustworthy NLP/AI, which often considers (neural)-symbolic approaches that are generalizable to complex reasoning scenarios. My approaches are often inspired by human cognitive processes such as analogy and experiential knowledge.

Prospective students: please send me an email if you are a MS/undergraduate student (any affiliation), or a PhD student already affiliated with ASU.
